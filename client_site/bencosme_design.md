# Profile of Your Client

## Who is your client?

**Client Name:** Devvin Sosa

Devvin is a thrift-shopping enthusiast who specializes in discovering funny, unusual, and eye‑catching articles of clothing. He curates these unique finds and wants a dedicated online space to showcase, post, and sell them. His brand centers on humor, individuality, and the thrill of finding something unexpected. He serves people who enjoy expressive fashion, vintage aesthetics, and clothing that sparks conversation.

## Mission Statement

**Mission Statement:**
To bring humor, personality, and individuality back into fashion by curating and selling one‑of‑a‑kind thrifted clothing that helps people express themselves without taking style too seriously.

## Goals for Creating the Website

* Create a centralized platform to post and sell curated thrift finds
* Build a recognizable personal brand around humor and unique fashion
* Make it easy for visitors to browse, purchase, and share items
* Eventually grow a loyal following through consistent drops and updates

## Existing Website

Devvin does **not** currently have a website.

# Profile of Target Audience

## Primary Audience Overview

The primary audience consists of young adults who enjoy expressive fashion, thrift culture, memes, and unique clothing pieces that stand out. They value individuality, humor, and affordability.

---

## Demographics

* **Age Range:** 18–30 years old
* **Gender Distribution:** Mixed, slightly skewed toward male and non‑binary audiences, but inclusive of all genders
* **Location:** Primarily United States
* **Living Area:** Mostly urban and suburban areas where thrift culture and streetwear trends are popular

---

## Socioeconomic Details

* **Average Income:** $1,000–$65,000 per year
* **Education Level:** High school diploma to college degree (many current college students or recent graduates)
* **Marital / Family Status:** Mostly single, some in relationships, few with children
* **Occupation:** Students, retail workers, creatives, freelancers, early‑career professionals
* **Average Work Hours:** 20–45 hours per week

---

## Web Behavior

* **Web Usage Frequency:** Heavy daily users; multiple hours per day
* **Devices Used:** Primarily smartphones, followed by laptops
